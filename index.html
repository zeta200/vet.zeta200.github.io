Welcome to Paws for Thoughts

Knowing the great bond which exists between owner an pet, we strive to treat all pets in our care with compassion, giving them the utmost care and attention at all times.

We have dedicated staff who are committed to exceptional client care, our clinical staff undergo CPD through the year to ensure they are up to date with new drugs and treatments.

Good parking facilities which makes your visits to us easier Dedicated reception staff Appointments and clinics with Nursing staff Puppy Cuddles and parties to help you and your pet in the first few months together Information and advise readily available for young, middle aged and older pets A scheme to save you money on your veterinary bills We look forward to seeing you.
